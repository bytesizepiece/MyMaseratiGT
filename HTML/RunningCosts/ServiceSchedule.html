Service Schedule


Service intervals are 2 years or 12,500 miles


A main dealer or specialist independent should be servicing the following items:



Every 2 years or 12,500 miles:

  • Oil (Shell Helix 5w/40 FS)
  • Oil Filter
  • Air Filter
  • Pollen Filter
  • Auxillary Belts
  • Brake Fluid Change
  • Coolant Change
  • Powersteering Fluid
  • Differential Fluid
  • Gearbox Fluid (F1 / MC Shift Gearbox only)


Every 4th year or 25,000 miles:

  • 2nd year service items plus
  • Carbon Canister
  • Lambda Sensors (x4)


Every 3rd Service or 37,500 miles:

  • Spark Plugs
