SportsMaserati.com

SportsMaserati.com is a UK focused user forum for all Maserati vehicles.

The forum has a very active user group, providing quick and detailed answers for even the most obscure questions relating to current or older Maserati models.

The forum owner (Matt) is also the owner of Sports Italia, which is a Hampshire based Maserati, Ferrari and Lamborghini independent service center.

MaseratiLife.com

Maserati Life is a more North American based Maserati owner forum with over 30,000 members.

A good resource for general information on Maserati cars, but for UK owners it might not be the best option for UK specific advise, such as sourcing parts.

Robin Elliott is a US based forum member who has helped develop and distribute the Aucar branded headunit and climate control upgrades. He has also created a number of videos showing the units in action and how to install.
