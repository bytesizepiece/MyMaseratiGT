There are lots of cars sold by good and honest used dealers, but it is worth remembering the Maserati Granturismo has specific servicing requirements beyond the usual oil and filter changes - Check the Service Schedule.

Many non-specialist dealers are also unaware of the consumable parts which can be expensive to replace, but they have not factored them into their pricing.

The advise is to remember the Granturismo is a £100,000 car when new and therefore has servicing costs associated with a car of that price range. Buy with caution, get the best car for your money and (hopefully) have an enjoyable ownership.

Take a look at the Buying Guide which gives a summary of some key points to look out for.

Having a pre-purchase inspection is highly recommended for any car purchase.
